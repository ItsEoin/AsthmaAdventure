Managing asthma should be child's play.

Everybody needs a little help sometimes, and we believe in the power of that extra push. Asthma Adventure transforms your little one's daily struggle with interactive games and collectible rewards.Get the app

It's not all fun and games, but we try our best.

Track Medication

By making use of a QR scanner, Asthma Adventure makes it easy to monitor your child's routine.

Play Games

Our interactive, fun games will help your child learn about their condition while enjoying new adventures.

Earn Trophies

Online and offline rewards help turn routine chores into healthy habits.
